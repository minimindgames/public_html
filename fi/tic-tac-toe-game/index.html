Tic Tac Toe rules

Tic Tac Toe rules are simple: Tic Tac Toe is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the squares in a 3x3 grid. The first player to mark a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line of three squares wins.

How to win Tic Tac Toe

  1. Win a line
  2. Block a line
  3. Make a fork
  4. Block a fork
  5. Exploit opening mistake
  6. Avoid opening mistake
