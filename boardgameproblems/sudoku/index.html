Sudoku problem

Fill in the next number.

To solve a Sudoku problem, you need to fill in just the next number instead of solving the complete Sudoku puzzle.

How to play

Sudoku puzzles in this game have one correct solution which can be reached by logic without any guessing.

You need to eliminate candidate numbers until some cell has just one possible number, which can be then filled in as a solution to the problem.

Hints and ads

The difficulty of problems varies between medium and extremely hard. You may unlock hints by watching an ad.
