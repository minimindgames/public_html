Super Tic Tac Toe

Challenging strategy game based on Tic Tac Toe.

The goal of Super Tic Tac Toe is to win three Tic Tac Toe boards in a row.

How to play

Win a game to raise a level and if you lose watch an ad to continue. The game gets more challenging on each level.

Super Tic Tac Toe rules

Super Tic Tac Toe has nine ordinary Tic Tac Toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid.

  • On the first move, the player is allowed to play on any square.
  • On the next moves, the player must always play on the BOARD, which matches position of the previously played SQUARE.
  • Note: If a board where you should play has already been resolved, you shall choose another board.
