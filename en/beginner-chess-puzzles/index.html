Use your chess skills to solve chess game puzzles. In this game, all puzzles are solved by checkmating in minimum moves.

Chess for beginners

How chess pawns move

The chess pawns move forward. Unlike other pieces, the pawns capture diagonally.

On the first move pawns can move up to two squares, but can also be captured in both squares.

How chess king moves

The chess king moves one square in any direction.

The king can't be in a square where it'd be captured.

How chess queen moves

The chess queen moves straight in any direction.

If a pawn moves to the last row, it can be promoted; In this game auto-promoted usually to a queen.

How chess rook moves

A chess rook moves orthogonally.

If a king and a rook have not moved yet, you can castle.

How chess bishop moves

The chess bishop moves diagonally.

Obviously, one bishop moves only in white squares and another in red squares.

How chess knight moves

The chess knight jumps two squares orthogonally and then one square sideways.

Unlike other pieces, the knight can jump over other pieces.
