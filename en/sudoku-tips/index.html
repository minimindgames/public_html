Sudoku Tips: How to solve Sudoku fast and fun Sudoku tips

Solve Sudoku fast

How to solve Sudoku in a minute? A fast way to solve Sudoku is to "scan" Sudoku by rows and columns to search for cells with only one possible number.

In the picture, you can see at a glance that number 1 fits just in one cell in the center box.

Sudoku dot-notation

Sudoku dot-notation is a convenient way to mark candidate numbers using just a dot instead of a number. Because dot is quick to mark (and scan), you can solve Sudoku puzzles much faster. In addition, a simple dot mark fits even on a small Sudoku grids.
