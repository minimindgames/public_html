Sudoku dot notation

Sudoku dot-notation is a convenient way to mark candidate numbers using just a dot instead of a number. Because dot is quick to mark (and scan), you can solve Sudoku puzzles much faster. In addition, a simple dot mark fits even on a small Sudoku grids.
