Chess problem

White to mate in two moves.

To solve a chess problem, you need to find just the next few moves instead of playing a complete game. A typical chess problem is to checkmate in N moves from a given chess position.

The chess problems in this game are from popular chess books, see credits.

How to play

You play with the white pieces and move first. You have only two moves to solve the problem.

Note: Computer may take up to a minute to make its move.

Hints and ads

The chess problems presented are challenging, but you may unlock a hint by watching an ad.

Chess rules

If you are not familiar with the "king" game yet, please see the chess rules.
