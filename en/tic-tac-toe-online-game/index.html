Tic Tac Toe rules

Tic Tac Toe is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the squares in a 3x3 grid. The first player to mark a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line of three squares wins.

  1. Win a line

  6. Avoid opening mistake
