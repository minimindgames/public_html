Super Tic Tac Toe

Super Tic Tac Toe is a challenging strategy game with the simple rules of Tic Tac Toe. Super Tic Tac Toe is also known as Mega Tic Tac Toe, Ultimate Tic Tac Toe and Nine Board Tic Tac Toe.

Super Tic Tac Toe rules

Super Tic Tac Toe has nine ordinary Tic Tac Toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid. Each board is played exactly like original Tic Tac Toe, but the trick is that you can't choose the board to play with (except on initial move).

First move

On the first move, a player is allowed to play on any of the nine boards.

In the example, X decided to play in the sixth cell of the second board (out of all 81 possible cells).

Next moves

The player must always play on the BOARD, which matches position of the previously played CELL (note that both are in a 3x3 grid).

In the example, X has played in the cell number six previously so O must play on the board number six (marked in yellow). Now, O decided to play in the middle cell so X will play next on the middle board.

Important additional rule: If a board where you are about to play has already been resolved, you may (and must) choose another (unresolved) board.

How to win

The first player, who gets three BOARDS in a line wins the game.

In this example game, O is playing on the yellow board but it really doesn't matter anymore. Wherever O plays, X can win the top-right board next (that is third in an up-right line) so X wins the whole game.

Super Tic Tac Toe has no definitive winning strategy, and I have very little to say about that. However, it's obvious that winning a single Tic Tac Toe board doesn't really mean much at all.
