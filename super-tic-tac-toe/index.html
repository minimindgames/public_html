Super Tic Tac Toe: Rules how to play and mobile Super Tic Tac Toe online

Super Tic Tac Toe is a challenging strategy game with the simple rules of Tic Tac Toe. Super Tic Tac Toe is known by many different names, including Mega Tic Tac Toe, Ultimate Tic Tac Toe and Tic Tac Toe on Nine Boards.

Super Tic Tac Toe

Super Tic Tac Toe rules

How to play Super Tic Tac Toe: Super Tic Tac Toe is like original Tic Tac Toe but with nine boards in a 3x3 grid. Notice that the nine boards are positioned in a 3x3 grid exactly as the cells on a Tic Tac Toe board.

First move

The starting player can first choose any of nine boards, and then play on that board like in the original Tic Tac Toe game.

Following moves

The next player can't choose freely which board to play on, but must play on a board matching a cell played on the previous turn. For example, if you play at the first cell on any of the nine boards the next player must play onto the first board.

In the example, X played first in the cell number six so O must play next on the board number six (in yellow). Because O decided to play in the middle cell, X will play next on the middle board.

Important additional rule: If a board was already resolved (won or tied), a player can't obviously play on that board so the player can choose freely any unresolved board.

Winning the game

The first player to get three boards in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line wins the game. Note that it's three boards instead of cells as compared to the original Tic Tac Toe game.

Super Tic Tac Toe has no winning strategy, unlike the original Tic Tac Toe game, and I have very little to say about that. However, it's obvious that winning a single Tic Tac Toe board doesn't really mean much at all.

Mobile Super Tic Tac Toe online

Mobile Super Tic Tac Toe is a simple point-and-click type of game, which runs online almost on any device with an internet browser, also on small mobile phones with a touch-screen.
